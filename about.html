Hello!

I’m Hongfei, a Game Design and Development graduate from Rochester Institute of Technology and currently a Master’s student in Data Science at the University of Virginia. Games were my entry point into tech. Designing mechanics, balancing systems, and building interactive experiences sparked my interest in how underlying data and logic shape player behavior and meaningful experiences.

Over time, that curiosity pulled me deeper into data science. My skill set has grown from C# and web development with CSS and JavaScript into Python, R, and the broader data science ecosystem. I enjoy working across the full pipeline, from cleaning and wrangling messy datasets to building models and translating results into clear, intuitive visualizations. I am especially drawn to using data to support game design decisions, system balancing, and player focused insights.

Outside of data and games, I enjoy working on cars, hiking, skiing, and cooking. These interests keep me curious and hands on, and they influence how I approach problem solving by balancing creativity, exploration, and technical precision.
