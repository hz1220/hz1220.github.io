About Me

Hongfei Zhu · Charlottesville, VA

Aspiring Data Scientist.

I'm Hongfei, currently pursuing my Master's in Data Science at the University of Virginia, with a B.S. in Game Design & Development from Rochester Institute of Technology. That combination isn't as strange as it sounds: game design is fundamentally about systems thinking, balance, and data-driven iteration, skills that transfer directly into how I approach analytical problems.

My data work centers on machine learning, statistical modeling, and building visualizations that make complex results actually understandable. I've applied ridge regression, KNN, logistic regression, K-means, and PCA to real-world datasets, and I care as much about communicating findings clearly as I do about the math underneath them.

Outside of data and code, I do 3D modeling, work on cars, and occasionally ship game jam projects, pursuits that keep my problem-solving grounded in craft and precision.
